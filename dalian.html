Key Fact
About Dalian

Name Origin

Dalian (大连) was named in 1899 during the Russian lease of the Liaodong Peninsula. The name means 'Great Link' and reflects the city's strategic location on the Yellow Sea. It was also known as Dairen during the Japanese period.

Education & Universities

Dalian is a significant education center in northeast China. Dalian University of Technology is one of China's top engineering universities, and Dalian Maritime University is the country's leading maritime university.

Metro & Transportation

Dalian Metro has 5 lines covering the city and connecting to the airport and railway stations. The hilly coastal roads and scenic Binhai Road make a private car the best way to enjoy the views and reach distant beaches.

Natural Resources & Environment

Dalian is located on the southern tip of the Liaodong Peninsula, surrounded by the Yellow Sea and Bohai Sea. The city is known for its clean environment, mild coastal climate, green hills, and numerous beaches and bays.

Getting to Dalian: Airports & Railway Stations

Everything you need to know about arriving in Dalian and reaching your hotel.

✈️ Airports

Dalian Zhoushuizi International Airport (DLC)

West of city center · 15 km

DLC is a major airport in northeast China with domestic and some international flights. A private car takes 30–45 minutes to downtown hotels.

Nearby Airports

Yantai Penglai, Qingdao

Yantai (YNT) and Qingdao (TAO) are alternative airports across the Bohai Sea. Dalian is connected to Yantai by ferry and to Qingdao by flight.

🚄 Railway Stations

Dalian Railway Station

City center · Zhongshan Square

The main station in the city center, serving high-speed trains to Shenyang, Harbin, and Beijing.

Dalian North Railway Station

Northern district · Main HSR

A major high-speed rail station serving long-distance trains to Beijing, Shenyang, and Harbin.

Jinzhou Railway Station

Jinzhou district · Suburban

Serves the northern Jinzhou district with conventional and high-speed trains.

Lüshun Railway Station

Lüshunkou · Southern tip

A small station serving the historic port area.

Dalian West Railway Station

Western suburbs · Freight

Mainly used for freight and some regional passenger services.

Food & Shopping in Dalian

🍽️ Must-Try Dalian Cuisine

Dalian Grilled Seafood

Fresh squid, scallops, oysters, and mussels grilled with garlic and spices. A beachside favorite.

Sea Cucumber (Hǎishēn)

Dalian is famous for high-quality sea cucumber, often braised in savory sauce. Considered a delicacy.

Stuffed Seafood Dumplings

Dumplings filled with fresh seafood like shrimp, scallop, or mackerel. Prized for thin skins and rich filling.

Grilled Cold Noodles (Kǎo Lěng Miàn)

A northeastern street snack of chewy cold noodles grilled with egg, sauce, and vegetables.

🛍️ Shopping Destinations

Intangible Cultural Heritage & Living Crafts of Dalian

Meet the traditional arts that have shaped Dalian's identity for centuries.

🎭 Dalian Yangge Folk Dance

A lively northeastern folk dance performed during festivals with drums and colorful costumes.

Where to find it: Spring festivals and public celebrations in parks and squares.

🎨 Lüshun Paper Cutting

A traditional folk art in the Lüshun area with intricate designs depicting local life and naval history.

Where to find it: Lüshunkou folk art shops and Dalian cultural markets.

🧵 Dalian Shell Carving

Artisans carve intricate designs into seashells to create decorative items and jewelry.

Where to find it: Souvenir shops along the Binhai Road and Xinghai Square.

🎵 Northeastern Errenzhuan

A comedic song-and-dance performance style popular in northeast China.

Where to find it: Local theaters and cultural performances in Dalian.

🏺 Dalian Glass Art

Dalian has a tradition of glassblowing and glass sculpture, producing decorative items and art pieces.

Where to find it: Art galleries and craft shops in the city center.

🪁 Seaside Kite Making

Traditional kite making with coastal themes like seagulls, ships, and fish.

Where to find it: Beach festivals and spring kite events.

Useful Chinese Phrases for Dalian

你好 (Nǐ hǎo)

Hello

谢谢 (Xièxiè)

Useful at restaurants.

Essential at tourist sites.
