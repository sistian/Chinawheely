Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ride?

Simply visit our booking page, fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation within 1 hour.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, Alipay, and major credit cards. Payment is required before service commencement.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes. Full refund for cancellations >24h before pickup. 50% refund for 12-24h. No refund for <12h.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, child seats are available upon request at no extra charge. Please mention this in your booking notes.
