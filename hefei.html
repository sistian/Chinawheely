Key Fact

Name Origin

Hefei (合肥) means 'Confluence of Two Rivers' or 'Lake Fei,' referring to the Fei and Shi Rivers that once joined here. The city has a history of over 2,200 years and was an important center in the feudal states of Chu and Wu before becoming the capital of Anhui province.

Education & Universities

Hefei is one of China's rising science and education cities. University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) is one of the country's top universities, especially for physics and technology. Hefei University of Technology and Anhui University are also major institutions.

Metro & Transportation

Hefei Metro has 5 lines covering the city center, Hefei South Railway Station, universities, and the western high-tech zone. The system is modern and expanding, but many scenic and business areas are best reached by car.

Natural Resources & Environment

Hefei lies between Chaohu Lake, one of China's largest freshwater lakes, and the low hills of central Anhui. The city has many parks and green corridors, with a humid subtropical climate and distinct seasons. It is known as a science city with a clean, modern environment.

Getting to Hefei: Airports & Railway Stations

Everything you need to know about arriving in Hefei and reaching your hotel.

✈️ Airports

Hefei Xinqiao International Airport (HFE)

Northwest of the city · 30 km to downtown

The main airport serving Hefei and Anhui province, with domestic and some international flights. A private car takes 40–60 minutes to downtown hotels.

Nearby Airports

Anqing Tianzhushan, Huangshan Tunxi, Chizhou Jiuhuashan

Regional airports serve scenic areas like Huangshan and Jiuhua Mountain. For Hefei itself, HFE is the main airport.

🚄 Railway Stations

Hefei South Railway Station

Main HSR hub · Baohe district

The primary high-speed rail station for Hefei, serving bullet trains to Shanghai, Nanjing, Wuhan, Hangzhou, and Beijing. It is one of the largest stations in central China.

Hefei Railway Station

Historic city center · Yaohai district

The older main station in the city center, serving conventional and some high-speed trains. Connected to Metro Line 1 and 3.

Hefei West Railway Station

New western hub · Shushan district

A newer station serving high-speed and suburban trains to the western districts and nearby mountain areas.

Hefei North Railway Station

Freight/suburban hub · North Hefei

Primarily a freight station, but also serves some suburban and regional passenger services.

Chaohu East Railway Station

Suburban station · Chaohu city

Serves high-speed trains on the Hefei-Nanjing and Hefei-Wuhan lines. Useful for trips to Chaohu Lake.

Food & Shopping in Hefei

🍽️ Must-Try Hefei Cuisine

Luzhou Roast Duck (Luzhou Ya)

A famous Anhui-style roast duck with crispy skin and tender meat. Hefei's signature dish, different from Beijing duck in flavor and preparation.

Sanhe Rice Dumplings (Sanhe Gao)

Sweet rice cakes from Sanhe Ancient Town, made with glutinous rice and local fillings. A popular snack and souvenir.

Anhui Stinky Mandarin Fish (Chou Guiyu)

A famous Anhui dish of fermented mandarin fish, pungent but delicious, often served in Huizhou-style restaurants.

Hefei Sesame Cakes

Crispy sesame-coated cakes, a popular street snack and breakfast item in Hefei.

Hefei Wanda Plaza

Baohe Park Night Market

Intangible Cultural Heritage & Living Crafts of Hefei

Meet the traditional arts that have shaped Hefei's identity for centuries.

🎭 Hui Opera (Anhui Opera)

One of the major traditional opera forms of China, known for its elegant singing, refined acting, and influence on Peking Opera. It originated in the Anhui region.

Where to find it: Local theaters and cultural festivals in Hefei.

🎨 Huizhou Wood Carving

Intricate carved woodwork from the Huizhou region, used in traditional architecture, furniture, and decorative items.

Where to find it: Craft shops and museums in Hefei.

🎋 Anhui Bamboo Weaving

Traditional weaving techniques using bamboo from the Dabie Mountains and Huizhou area, creating baskets, screens, and art.

Where to find it: Local craft markets and cultural fairs.

🖌️ Huizhou Seal Carving

The refined art of carving seals from local stone, a respected tradition among scholars and artists.

Where to find it: Calligraphy shops and art markets in Hefei.

🎶 Anhui Folk Songs

Traditional folk music from the rural regions of Anhui, often sung while working in the fields or tea plantations.

Where to find it: Folk festivals and community events.

✂️ Anhui Paper Cutting

Delicate paper-cut art with themes from opera, nature, and local legends, used for festivals and decorations.

Where to find it: Folk art shops and cultural fairs.

Best Time to Visit Hefei

Plan your trip around Hefei's seasons for the best experience.

🌸

Spring (Mar–May)

12–22°C · Mild & Rainy

Pleasant and green, with flowers blooming in the parks. Rain is common, so bring an umbrella.

☀️

Summer (Jun–Aug)

25–34°C · Hot & Humid

Hot and humid, with occasional heavy rain. Early mornings and evenings are best for sightseeing.

🍂

Autumn (Sep–Nov)

15–25°C · Cool & Clear

The best time to visit. Comfortable temperatures, clear skies, and good conditions for mountain trips.

❄️

Winter (Dec–Feb)

0–8°C · Cold & Damp

Chilly and damp, but milder than northern China. Museums and hot pot are good indoor escapes.
