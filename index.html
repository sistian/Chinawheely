FAQs About Private Car Service in China

Common questions about booking private car and airport transfer in China. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We track your flight in real-time. For airport pickups, we include 60 minutes (Economy) or 90 minutes (Business/MPV) of free waiting time after your scheduled landing. If your flight is delayed, your driver will be automatically notified and will wait — no extra charge.

Do your drivers speak English?

Our drivers are skilled at using translation apps and AI tools to communicate effectively with international guests. While they may not be fluent in English, they are trained to bridge language barriers seamlessly.

If you need fluent English communication, we offer two guide options as add-ons:

  • Escort Guide — Basic English, assists with logistics and navigation. Available in all cities.
  • Expert Guide — Fluent English with deep cultural/historical knowledge. Available in major cities (Beijing, Shanghai, Xi'an, Chengdu, Guangzhou).

Guide pricing varies by city. You'll see the option during booking.

Can I book for multiple cities in one trip?

Absolutely. You can book each leg of your journey separately, or contact us for a multi-city itinerary. We offer discounts for bookings of 3 or more rides. For complex trips, email us at 15527771775@qq.com or WhatsApp +86-15527771775 and we'll build a custom package.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24-48 hours receive a 50% refund. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable, but we can reschedule at no extra cost if you contact us at least 4 hours before pickup. Contact: 15527771775@qq.com or +86-15527771775 (WhatsApp).

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal and Alipay. All Chinese domestic payments are settled in CNY. International payments via PayPal are processed in USD. You'll receive an invoice by email after your ride.

Are child seats available?

Child and baby seats are available with 96 hours advance notice. This includes cleaning and disinfection. Cost: CNY 200 per seat. Please indicate your child's age when booking so we can provide the appropriate seat type.

Do you provide business/corporate accounts?

Yes, we offer corporate accounts with monthly billing, dedicated account managers, and priority booking. Contact us at 15527771775@qq.com or WhatsApp +86-15527771775 for a tailored proposal.
