Frequently Asked Questions About Our Services

What services does Chinawheely offer?

We offer airport transfers, city car charter, intercity trips, corporate travel, and custom sightseeing tours across 40+ cities in China. All services include a professional English-speaking driver, fuel, tolls, and parking.

How do I book a private car in China?

Visit our booking page, select your service type, enter your pickup and dropoff details, choose a vehicle, and submit. We will confirm your booking within 1 hour via WhatsApp or email.

Do your drivers speak English?

Yes. All Chinawheely drivers are fluent in English and Mandarin. They are trained to communicate clearly with international travelers, help with luggage, and provide local recommendations.

What is included in the price?

Our fixed price includes the vehicle, driver, fuel, tolls, parking, and up to 60 minutes of free waiting time for airport pickups. There are no hidden fees or surge charges.

Can I book a car for a whole day?

Yes. We offer 4-hour, 8-hour, and 12-hour city charter packages. You can customize the itinerary with multiple stops within the city or nearby attractions.
