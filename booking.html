Frequently Asked Questions About Booking

Do I need to pay in advance?

We ask for a small deposit or full payment to confirm your booking. You can pay via PayPal or Alipay, and we'll send confirmation within 10 minutes.

What happens if my flight is delayed?

We track your flight in real time. For reasonable delays, your driver will wait at no extra charge. Just make sure your flight number is correct.

Can I book a car for a full day?

Yes. Select "City / Hourly" service and choose 4, 8, or 12 hours. Your driver will follow your itinerary for the day.

Is the price per person or per car?

All prices are per car, not per person. The vehicle capacity is shown next to each option (Economy, Business, MPV).
