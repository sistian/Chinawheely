Name Origin

The name "Chengdu" (成都) means "Becoming a Capital." According to legend, the city was named after the ancient Shu king who declared "one year to build, two years to settle, three years to become a capital." It has served as the capital of Sichuan Province for over 2,300 years. Known as the "Land of Abundance" (天府之国), Chengdu sits on the fertile Chengdu Plain, fed by the Dujiangyan irrigation system built in 256 BC.

Education & Universities

Chengdu is western China's educational hub with over 60 universities. Sichuan University (founded 1896, ranked top 50 in Asia) and University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) (one of China's "Seven Sons of National Defence") are the most prestigious. The city is also home to the Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding, one of the world's leading panda research centers.

Metro & Transportation

Chengdu's metro system has 13 lines covering 500+ km, making it one of China's fastest-growing networks. The airport line connects Tianfu Airport (TFU) to the city center in 45 minutes. However, the Panda Base and Dujiangyan are not reachable by metro. Our private car service is the most convenient way to visit these suburban attractions, especially with a knowledgeable driver who knows the best routes.

Natural Resources & Environment

Chengdu sits on the fertile Chengdu Plain, surrounded by the Longmen Mountains to the west and the Qionglai Mountains to the southwest. The Dujiangyan irrigation system, built 2,200 years ago, still diverts water from the Min River to the plain. The city is famous for its laid-back lifestyle, with more teahouses than any other city in the world. The surrounding mountains are a biodiversity hotspot, home to the giant panda and golden snub-nosed monkey.

Top Chengdu Attractions

Discover the treasures of Sichuan's capital with a private driver.

🐼

Giant Panda Base

Research Base • 80+ Pandas

The Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding is home to 80+ pandas, including adorable cubs. Located 10 km north of the city, it's the world's best place to see pandas in a naturalistic habitat. Visit early morning (8–10 AM) when pandas are most active. Our driver knows the best times to avoid crowds.

🏛️

Wuhou Shrine & Jinli Street

Three Kingdoms • Ancient Temple

A memorial temple dedicated to Zhuge Liang, the legendary strategist of the Three Kingdoms period. The adjacent Jinli Ancient Street is a beautifully restored pedestrian area with traditional architecture, tea houses, and street food. Your driver can drop you at the main entrance and suggest the best local snacks.

🌊

Dujiangyan Irrigation System

UNESCO World Heritage • 2,200 Years Old

Built in 256 BC, this ancient irrigation system still diverts water from the Min River to the Chengdu Plain. A marvel of ancient engineering that transformed Sichuan into the "Land of Abundance." Located 60 km from Chengdu, it's a perfect half-day trip with a private car.

⛩️

Mount Qingcheng

Taoist Mountain • UNESCO World Heritage

One of the birthplaces of Taoism, with ancient temples nestled among lush forests. Known as the "Most Tranquil Mountain Under Heaven." Located 65 km from Chengdu, it's a perfect day trip combining nature and culture. Your driver can take you to the front mountain entrance and wait while you hike.

🎭

Sichuan Opera & Face Changing

Traditional Art • Face Changing

Sichuan Opera is famous for its face-changing technique (Bian Lian), where performers switch masks in the blink of an eye. The Shu Feng Ya Yun Tea House near Wuhou Shrine offers the most authentic performances. Your driver can arrange tickets and drop you at the venue.

🌿

People's Park & Tea Houses

Tianfu Square • Chengdu Lifestyle

The heart of Chengdu's laid-back culture. People's Park is filled with teahouses where locals play mahjong, practice tai chi, and enjoy ear cleaning (a traditional Chengdu service). The Heming Teahouse is the most famous. Your driver can drop you off and pick you up after you've experienced authentic Chengdu tea culture.

Food & Shopping in Chengdu

🍽️ Must-Try Chengdu Cuisine

Sichuan Hotpot (Chuan Chuan Xiang)

Chengdu's most famous food experience. A bubbling pot of spicy chili oil broth where you cook thinly sliced meat, vegetables, and tofu. The numbing sensation from Sichuan peppercorns (málà) is addictive. Your driver can recommend the best local hotpot spot away from tourist traps.

Mapo Tofu

Silky tofu in a spicy sauce of fermented black beans, chili oil, and minced pork. Created in Chengdu over 150 years ago. The authentic version is much spicier than what you find abroad. Your driver knows the best Mapo Tofu restaurant near People's Park.

Dan Dan Noodles

Chewy noodles tossed in a spicy sauce of chili oil, Sichuan peppercorns, preserved vegetables, and minced pork. Named after the pole (dan dan) that street vendors carried. A Chengdu street food classic.

Rabbit Heads (Tu Tou)

A Chengdu delicacy that adventurous foodies must try. Spiced rabbit heads are braised and then deep-fried, creating a crispy, flavorful snack. The locals say "no rabbit can leave Chengdu alive" because they're so beloved. Your driver can find the best vendor.

🛍️ Shopping Destinations

Chunxi Road

Chengdu's most famous shopping street, with international brands, local boutiques, and street food. The pedestrian area is always bustling. Your driver can drop you at the main entrance and pick you up after shopping.

Jinli Ancient Street

A beautifully restored ancient street with traditional architecture, handicrafts, and street food. Perfect for Sichuan opera masks, bamboo carvings, and tea. Your driver can drop you at the entrance and suggest the best local snacks.

Wide & Narrow Alleys (Kuanzhai Xiangzi)

Three parallel alleys (Wide, Narrow, and Well) restored from Qing Dynasty architecture. Filled with boutiques, art galleries, teahouses, and upscale restaurants. A great place to experience Chengdu's traditional culture with a modern twist.

Chengdu International Finance Square (IFS)

Chengdu's premier luxury shopping destination with international brands, a rooftop garden with a giant panda sculpture, and gourmet restaurants. Your driver can pick you up after shopping and take you directly to the airport or your hotel.
